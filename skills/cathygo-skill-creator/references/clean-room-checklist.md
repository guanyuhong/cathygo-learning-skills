# Clean-room Checklist

Before publishing a Skill, confirm:

- The Skill contains no textbook PDFs, screenshots, scans, or photos.
- Explanations are written in CathyGO's own words.
- Assessment items are original.
- Example numbers, names, and scenarios are original.
- Comic characters and scenes are original.
- No publisher artwork, page layout, or answer key is included.
- General facts are stated without copying a source's expression.
